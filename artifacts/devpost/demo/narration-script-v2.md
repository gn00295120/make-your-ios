# MakeYour — OpenAI Build Week demo v2 narration

Target: English, 2:04.20, public YouTube upload. This revision keeps
the original product tour and customization story while replacing the short
creation beat with the complete TripPilot prompt, accelerated generation,
automatic validator-guided repair, capability review, and working currency
runtime.

## 1. Three pages, one home — 0:00

This is MakeYour, one iPhone app where GPT-5.6 creates the tiny apps you need.
My Apps keeps every creation. Builder is where you create or change them. AI Key
connects your own OpenAI key and GPT-5.6. Together, they become one home for
your personal tiny apps.

## 2. Create and repair TripPilot — 0:21

Now I ask GPT-5.6 for TripPilot, a three-page travel command center with
currency, budget, itinerary, device tools, and AI. This is the real generation
run; only the waiting time is accelerated. The first document fails semantic
validation. Instead of timing out or asking me to start over, MakeYour keeps my
prompt, sends precise diagnostics back to GPT-5.6, and automatically repairs
revision one. After capability review, the validated app opens with thirty-one
native components and twenty-one reviewed capabilities. Its generated converter
works too: one hundred U.S. dollars becomes three thousand two hundred fifty
Taiwan dollars. That entire interface came from the prompt, not a hard-coded
TripPilot template.

## 3. Make it your style — 1:06

A useful app should also feel like yours. I complete a task, return to Builder,
and ask for a calm green design with a short evening reflection. GPT-5.6 creates
Version 3 with a new style and a new feature, while my completed task is still
there. The app changes with me, without starting over.

## 4. Many tiny apps, one home — 1:26

Back in My Apps, Daily Focus is only one of many. I can switch to a travel budget
converter, live currency alerts, or a private food journal. Every tiny app can
have its own interface, capabilities, and data, while they all live inside
MakeYour. No new Xcode project. No separate build pipeline. No starting from
scratch.

## 5. Built with Codex — 1:49

Codex helped us build and test the schema, validator, safety boundary, and native
SwiftUI runtime that make this possible.

## 6. Close — 1:57

The next tiny app you need is one prompt away. One app. Your tiny apps. Make
yours.
